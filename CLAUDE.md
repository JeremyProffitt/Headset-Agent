## Project Overview

Voice-based headset troubleshooting agent with programmable personas.

## Key Documentation

- `docs/persona-troubleshooting-guide.md` - Personas (Tangerine, Joseph, Jennifer) and troubleshooting methodology
- `knowledge-base/` - Headset troubleshooting documentation

## Personas

Three distinct agent personalities deliver the same troubleshooting expertise:
- **Tangerine** - Irish, upbeat and enthusiastic
- **Joseph** - Ohio engineer, calm and patient
- **Jennifer** - Nebraska farm girl, fast-talking and folksy
